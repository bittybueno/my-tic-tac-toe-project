Accessible Tic Tac Toe

This game of Tic Tac Toe is made to be played with a screen reader. To travel through the board, press [ TAB ]. To reverse through the board, press [ SHIFT ] + [ TAB ]. To select a spot, press [ SPACE ]. The first player to get 3 marks in a row wins!

Player 1 is up.

Player 1 is X. Player 2 is O. Player 1 is currently not on a square.
